What is medical record abstraction?

They are companies that provide the expertise and resources necessary to extract relevant data from medical records and translate that data into a standardized format.

Why would I need abstraction services for medical records?

Abstraction services are typically needed when large amounts of medical data need to be collected and analyzed quickly and accurately. This is often the case in clinical research or quality improvement projects.

How are medical record abstraction different from medical coding services?

While medical coding services also involve translating medical data into a standardized format, they focus primarily on assigning codes to diagnoses and procedures for billing purposes. These abstraction services, on the other hand, focus on extracting relevant data for clinical research or quality improvement projects.

How do such services ensure the accuracy of the data they extract?

The services use trained professionals who follow standardized protocols to ensure the accuracy and completeness of the data they extract. They may also use automated tools to check for errors or inconsistencies.

What services do outsourced medical record abstraction companies typically offer?

Medical record abstraction firms typically offer services to extract, review, and summarize critical patient data from medical records. These services can include data extraction, coding, quality checks, and analysis to support research, clinical trials, quality improvement, and payer activities. Healthcare organizations outsource abstraction services for medical records to reduce costs and improve efficiency.
