Our End-to-End Order Management Support Process

Our order management process is meticulously designed to streamline the end-to-end handling of customer orders, ensuring accuracy, timeliness, and customer satisfaction. This process comprises

  • 1

    Order Processing

    Our team receives incoming orders and records essential details such as product, quantity, and delivery information.

  • 2

    Inventory Check

    During the order processing stage we simultaneously carry out a concomitant inventory check to verify product availability and update the customer.

  • 3

    Payment Processing

    We facilitate secure and hassle-free payment processing for the orders.


  • 4

    Order Fulfillment

    In this stage the gathered information is passed on to the inventory department to process orders for shipments as per customer specifications.

  • 5

    Shipping Coordination

    The inventory team coordinates with the logistics team to arrange for timely and efficient shipping.

  • 6

    Order Tracking

    Next, we provide customers with order tracking information to ensure customers can check order status anytime.

  • 7

    Returns and Exchanges

    In the post-delivery stage, our team handles customer returns or exchanges, to resolve issues quickly.

1. What is Order Management?

It is the process of managing and tracking the life cycle of an order, from the time a customer places it to when it is fulfilled and delivered. This includes processing payments and managing inventory, coordinating with suppliers and shipping providers, and tracking the status of orders. Order management systems are used by businesses to automate and streamline these processes and improve efficiency.

2. Why should I outsource order management services?

Order management outsourcing services help businesses streamline their operations and improve efficiency. It allows businesses to focus on their core competencies while a team of professionals handles the day-to-day processes of managing orders. Outsourcing order fulfillment services can also be a cost-effective solution for your business.
