Invensis Technologies to Exhibit Back Office Services Portfolio at the 41st NCBFAA Annual Conference, April 19 to 23, Orlando, 2015

As an exhibitor at the annual conference, the BPO services company will showcase its suite of back office services for customs brokerage and logistics BPO support for organizations seeking solutions for their business challenges.

Bangalore, Karnataka, India, April 3, 2015 – Invensis Technologies, a leading BPO services company, will participate as an Exhibitor at the 41st NCBFAA Annual Conference to be held from April 19-23, 2015, in Orlando, Florida. This is the first time the company is participating at the annual conference, which is a platform that brings together over 600 of the US’ leading customs brokers, freight forwarders, NVOCCs (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers), OTI (Ocean Transport Intermediaries) and other service providers.

As an exhibitor at the conference, Invensis will showcase its portfolio of end-to-end back office services for customs brokerage and logistics BPO support that solve the critical business requirements of organizations in the domain. The company has more than 12 years of experience in delivering tailored back office services to global customs brokers, freight forwarders and logistics providers. By leveraging Invensis’ in-depth understanding of the processes, best practices and regulatory standards of the customs brokerage and logistics industry, and Customs Specialist Certified expertise, organizations can witness the benefits of outsourcing to an expert. These benefits include: increased accuracy and turnaround time in data processing, avoidance of penalty payments, timely transmission of processed data to regulatory authorities, data security, accuracy of data entry, always-on, scalable operations, and more.

Speaking about Invensis’ participation at the conference, Vara Prasad Rongala, Managing Director, Invensis Technologies, said, “The NCBFAA Annual Conference is a unique platform that brings together leading organizations in the US customs brokerage and logistics industry. Invensis looks forward to the opportunity to understand the back office business challenges of these organizations, and enabling them to solve these through our end-to-end outsourcing services.”

The 41st NCBFAA Annual Conference is scheduled to be held from April 19-23 at Hilton Orlando Bonnet Creek, Orlando, Florida. The theme for this year is ‘Converging Logistics: Realities vs. Possibilities’.
